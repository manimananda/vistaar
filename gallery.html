The Techno-Cultural fest “VISTAAR” was Inaugurated by Dr. Prof. Satyabrata Mohanta , Honourable Director, I.G.I.T. Sarang, Dr. Prof. Urmila Bhanja, Head Of the Department, Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering. Dr. S.K. Dogra, Chief Manager, Nalco was the chief guest in this inaugural event. The evening began on a bright note with the welcoming of the guests and lighting of the lamp.

Talk

VISTAAR conducted a Talk on “Entrepreneurship”. The talk was delivered by our Chief Guest Dr. S.K. Dogra, Chief Manager, Nalco. He motivated all the students through his speech and gave a brief Idea on the Importance of Entrepreneurship to Society. The talk was conducted by all the faculty members and the students of ETC Department.

Electronica Quest

  • This event gave a tantalizing mix of knowledge and application of spontaneous skills, all at the speed of thought. For the smartest minds, there was victory and pride as reward. This quiz provides an extravaganza in electronics and communication.
  • The event consists of 2 rounds, in which the 1st round had a paper-pen based quiz and puzzle solving round and the 2nd round was a hunt round which was based on electronic riddles.

Seminar Presentation

VISTAAR conducted a Seminar Presentation on “Machine Learning” , “Optical Computers” and “Wireless Power Transmission”. The participants exhibit their knowledge on the topic with the help of slides which was judged by an experienced panel of judges.

Fest Guests

The highlight of the fest was the Guest lecture by Foreign Faculty Proff. FRANK.B.BROKKEN from Netherland who motivated the students through his valuable speech and gave a brief idea on “Information Security”.

Circuitrix

In this event, the participants solved some questions based on circuits and on next round a circuit was practically designed by the qualifiers based on visuals.

Flash Mob

The “Flash Mob” was Organised by the team VISTAAR inside the college campus. It was a public performance in which many groups participated and showed their fantastic performances.

Star Night

Star Night with renowned Singer Amrita Nayak who entertained the students with her melodious voice.

EDM night

DJ Neel and DJ Rahul thrilled the show and makes the atmosphere energetic with the thumping EDM and the tapping foot.
